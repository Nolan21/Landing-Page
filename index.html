This is our Solar System

The solar system is a vast area of our local planetary neighbors: the Sun, Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune.

Spotlight: The Sun, and the First Three

TheSunis the largest planetary body in the Solar System. In fact, it contains about 99.8% of the mass of the entire solar system.

Mercury is the first planet closest to the Sun. It is also the smallest planet in the Solar System. Also, it is shrinking!

Venus is the second planet closest to the Sun. It is hotter than Mercury despite being farther away from the Sun.

Earth is the third planet closest to the Sun. 70% of Earth's surface is covered in water.
